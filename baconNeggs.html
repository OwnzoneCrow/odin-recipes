Bacon and Eggs

For making this meal we will need to use a pan, a fork, and/or a spatula. Our ingredients are as followed:

  • Bacon (any amount)
  • Eggs (any amount)
  • Salt and Pepper

We will create our simple breakfast in the following steps:

  1. Insert the bacon inside the pan.
  2. Flip the bacon when browning continues and cook to your liking. (The longer it cooks, the darker/harder it gets)
  3. Take the bacon out of the pan and do NOT drain/clean the pan. We will use the grease for our eggs.
  4. Crack the eggs into the pan. Avoid getting any eggshells inside the pan.
  5. Whisk the eggs inside the pan and make sure they are completely liquified into a yellowish paste.
  6. From here will use you spatula or fork to move the eggs around. We want to avoid the eggs sticking on to the pan. The eggs will cook quickly.
  7. During or after the eggs are cooking, feel free to use salt and pepper to give flavor. With this, you are able to enjoy your meal!

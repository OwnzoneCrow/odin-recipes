Gringo Tacos

For this recipe we will need to use a pan and many bowls and plates. While this style of tacos are not the best, it's pretty easy to do and available everywhere.

Our ingredients are as followed (for 2 people):

  • 500g of ground beef or beef/pork mix
  • 10 flour tortillas
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • Half a cup to 1 cup of water
  • Olive Oil

Additional toppings are great as well, here are some examples:

  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Avocado
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream

For cooking our Gringo Tacos we will do the following

  1. Put a small amount of olive oil in the pan and heat the pan for about 5 minutes. Make sure to have the oil move around the pan.
  2. Insert the meat into the pan. Stir and ground the meat from time to time and wait until it is cooked.
  3. Drain the liquid from the pan.
  4. Insert the taco seasoning and the water. Make sure the water covers most of the meat.
  5. Stir the meat again and wait for the water to boil. This takes about 10-15 minutes on medium heat
  6. We will drain our pan again, afterwards we are able to use the meat in our tacos.
  7. Add any extra toppings to your tacos and enjoy!

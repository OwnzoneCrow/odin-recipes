Cheap Instant Ramen

For making this ramen we will need to use a plastic bowl and a microwave/range to heat the noodles. Making this ramen is so easy even a child can do this with no problems. Our ingredients are as followed:

  • Ramen Noodles (as seen above)
  • Water (approxiamtely 400-500 mL depending on the noodles)
  • Soup Powder (which is located along with the noodles)

We will create our delicious but super cheap ramen in the following steps:

  1. Open the noodle package and insert the noodles inside the bowl.
  2. Rip open the soup spice packet and pour to the side of the bowl so it will go to the bottom.
  3. Pour about 400-500 mL of water inside the bowl. If the noodles are broken up, pour around 400.
  4. Set the lid on the bowl and insert into the microwave and set the wattage to 500 for 7 minutes.
  5. Take the lid off and add any extra toppings and enjoy!
